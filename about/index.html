Project

The aim of this project is to display the possible direct bus routes from a given stop.

Data

At the moment, the only displayed data comes from BlaBlaBus theoretical timetables of the European network and from Flixbus theoretical timetables of the European network. The data was scrapped on September 2nd, 2025 and September 5th, 2025, respectfully.
